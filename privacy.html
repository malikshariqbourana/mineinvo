Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information MineInvo ("we," "our," "the site") collects when you use mineinvo.pages.dev, and how that information is used.

1. Invoice data stays in your browser

MineInvo is a client-side tool. The business details, client details, line items, and other invoice information you enter are processed entirely within your own browser to generate your invoice. We do not operate a server that stores, reads, or has access to the content of your invoices.

If you use the "Save Draft" feature, that data is stored so you can return to it later. It is tied to your own session and is not shared with other users or used by us for any purpose.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use MineInvo (for example, which pages are viewed and how long visitors stay). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this information, which may include your IP address, browser type, and general location. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

MineInvo may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or similar advertising networks. These networks may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

Cookies used on this site are limited to analytics and advertising purposes described above. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time; doing so may affect how ads and analytics function but will not affect your ability to create and download invoices.

5. Third-party links

MineInvo may contain links to third-party sites (such as this Privacy Policy's references to Google). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

6. Children's privacy

MineInvo is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
